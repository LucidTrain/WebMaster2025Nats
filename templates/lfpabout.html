Our Story

Founded in 1925, La Fettuccine Papa has been serving authentic Italian cuisine with a side of respect and loyalty. Our family business operates on the principles that have guided generations: quality ingredients, traditional recipes, and an atmosphere where guests are treated like famiglia.

Just as a good consigliere never betrays their don, we never compromise on our standards. Our kitchen is our territory, and we protect it with unwavering dedication to culinary excellence. When you dine with us, you're not just a customer – you're under our protection for the evening.

From our secret family recipes to the way we handle business, everything at La Fettuccine Papa is done with honor and respect for tradition. As we like to say, "Leave the gun, take the cannoli."
